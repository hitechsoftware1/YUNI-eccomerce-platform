# **App Name**: YUNI

## Core Features:

- Product Browsing: Browse products by category or search.
- Product Details: View product details with images and descriptions.
- Product Filtering: Filter products based on price, brand, or rating.
- Product Recommendations: AI-powered tool to recommend relevant products based on browsing history and search queries.
- Reviews and Ratings: Display customer product reviews and ratings.
- Search Functionality: Secure search functionality.
- Banners: Display of promotional banners.
- Lots of Categories: Display of many product categories.
- Cool Navigation Bars: Cool navigation bars.
- Perfect User Interface: Perfect User Interface.
- Top Banner Image Slider: Full-width image slider with smooth transitions showcasing popular ads, products, or events.
- Flash Sales Section: A flash sales section with a red countdown timer and horizontally scrollable product cards.
- Product Category Sections: Horizontally scrollable product lineups for different product categories.
- Product Categories Grid: A neat grid of circular icons showing categories.
- Latest Products & Listings: Grid layout of the most recent uploads with product details and hover effects.
- Animated Banner Message: Bouncing banner in yellow with a welcome message.
- Promo Grid with Filters: Promo section with filter buttons showing image or video cards with captions.
- About YUNI Section: Information about YUNI with contact details.
- Install Prompt (PWA): Black banner sliding in with an install prompt for the PWA.
- Bottom Navigation Bar: Bottom navigation bar fixed at the bottom of the screen.
- Modals (Popups): Popups with input forms for login/register and posting an ad.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant orange (#FF8C00) to represent energy, enthusiasm, and warmth, creating a welcoming and exciting shopping environment.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0), a neutral backdrop that ensures product images and descriptions stand out.
- Accent color: Yellow-orange (#FFA547) to guide user attention to key interactive elements and important information.
- Headline font: 'Poppins' (sans-serif) for headlines. Its modern and geometric structure makes it ideal for drawing attention to headings and key information.
- Body font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for the body text. It's clean and legible which is necessary to aid reading longer text segments.
- Use clear and simple icons for navigation and product categories.
- A clean, grid-based layout for displaying products. Prioritize high-quality product images.
